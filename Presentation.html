AQR - Quality Minus Junk

Working paper written by Clifford S. Asness, Andrea Frazzini, and Lasse H. Pedersen

Evaluated the performance of “quality” stocks vs “junk” stocks

Quality refers to companies that are:

  • Profitable
  • Growing
  • Well-Managed

Strategy:

  • Long high quality
  • Short low quality
